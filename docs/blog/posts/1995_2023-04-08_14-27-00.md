<h1 id="subject_show">Kōin Ya no Gotoshi (光陰矢の如し - Time Flies)</h1>
<div class="date" hidden>Apr 8, 2023 14:27</div>
<div id="post"><div id="body_show_ori">
Kōin Ya no Gotoshi<br/><br/>I am now 35 years old.<br/><br/>I feel that time has passed very quickly in the last few years.<br/><br/>It is exactly like 'kōin ya no gotoshi' (光陰矢の如し).<br/><br/>'Kō' (光) means "light" and 'in' (陰) means "shade," but here they are used as metaphors for "sun" and "moon" (or "day" and "night"), respectively.<br/><br/>In addition, 'ya' (矢) means "arrow," and 'gotoshi' (如し) means "like" or "as if."<br/><br/>In other words, this idiom means that time flies as quickly as an arrow.
</div></div>
