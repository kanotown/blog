<h1 id="subject_show">Ichaicha (いちゃいちゃ - Lovey-Dovey)</h1>
<div class="date" hidden>Dec 31, 2018 18:38</div>
<div id="post"><div id="body_show_ori">
Ichaicha<br/><br/>Yesterday, I introduced you to the Japanese term 'chinchin kamokamo' (ちんちんかもかも), which means that a woman and a man snuggle each other.<br/><br/>There is another term, 'ichaicha' (いちゃいちゃ), which has a similar meaning to 'chinchin kamokamo', and is more commonly used.<br/><br/>Originally, 'ichaicha' was used to mean to argue, but later it has come to mean to make out or snuggle.<br/><br/>Usually, this term is used as 'ichaicha suru' (いちゃいちゃする) by adding 'suru' (する - meaning "do").<br/><br/>Or its verb form 'icha tsuku' (いちゃつく) is used instead.
</div></div>
