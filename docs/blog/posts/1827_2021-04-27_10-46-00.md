<h1 id="subject_show">Furyō (不良 - Bad State/Boy/Girl)</h1>
<div class="date" hidden>Apr 27, 2021 10:46</div>
<div id="post"><div id="body_show_ori">
Furyō<br/><br/>When a thing or situation is bad, we use the term 'furyō' (不良) to describe its state.<br/><br/>Since 'fu' (不) means "not" and 'ryō' (良) means "good," the literal meaning of 'furyō' is "not good."<br/><br/>This term is often used as a short for 'furyō kōi shōnen' (不良行為少年).<br/><br/>Since 'kōi' (行為) means "action" and 'shōnen' (少年) means "boy," 'furyō kōi shōnen' literally means "a boy (or a girl) who taking bad actions."<br/><br/>It can be simply translated into English as "bad boy" or "bad girl."
</div></div>

不良<br/><br/>ものや状況が悪い状態のことを、日本語で「不良」と言います。<br/><br/>「不」は "not"、「良」は "good" を意味するので、「不良」の文字どおりの意味は "not good" となります。<br/><br/>この言葉は、「不良行為少年」の略称としてもよく使われます。<br/><br/>「行為」は "action"、「少年」は "boy" を意味するので、「不良行為少年」は文字どおり "a boy (or a girl) who taking bad actions" という意味になります。<br/><br/>英語では単に "bad boy" や "bad girl" と呼ばれます。
