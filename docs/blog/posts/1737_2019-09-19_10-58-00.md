Interesting Riddles Part 3<br/><br/>Today I will give you two interesting Japanese 'nazonazo' (なぞなぞ - "Riddle").<br/><br/>Q1: 'Machigai darake no mēru wa nanji ni todoku?' (間違いだらけのメールは何時に届く？ - "What time does a corrupt e-mail arrive?")<br/><br/>Q2: 'Sekai no chūshin ni iru mushi wa?' (世界の中心にいる虫は？ - "What a kind of insect that is at the center of the world?")<br/><br/>////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////<br/><br/>The answer to Q1 is 'goji' (5時 - "5 o'clock").<br/><br/>Because 'goji' can be written in kanji as 誤字, which means "typo."<br/><br/>The answer to Q2 is 'ka' (蚊 - "mosquito").<br/><br/>Because the central character of 'sekai' (世界/せかい - "world") is 'ka' (か - "mosquito").
