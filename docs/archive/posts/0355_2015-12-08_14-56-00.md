# Ground Beetle
<div class="date">Dec 8, 2015 14:56</div>
<div id="post"><div id="body_show_ori">
Do you know how to say "ground beetle" in Japanese?<br/><br/>It's "ゴミムシ (gomimushi; means garbage insect)," because we can often see them in garbage pits.<br/><br/>It's a little pitiful name, and is used as a calumny.<br/><br/>In particular, "メクラチビゴミムシ(mekura-chibi-gomimushi; means sightness dumpling garbage insect)," which is a kind of ground beetle, is awful name.<br/><br/>For a long time, Japanese entomology researchers have discussed whether "ゴミムシ" should be renamed or not.
</div></div>

<!-- more -->

<div id="post_ja"><div id="body_show_mo">
ゴミムシ<br/><br/>「Ground Beetle」の日本語名を知っていますか？<br/><br/>ゴミ溜めで見かけることが多いことに由来して、「ゴミムシ」と呼ばれます。<br/><br/>ちょっとかわいそうな名前で、悪口としても使われます。<br/><br/>特にゴミムシの中の「メクラチビゴミムシ」はひどい名前だと思います。<br/><br/>現在日本の昆虫学研究者の間で、改名すべきか議論がなされています。
</div></div>
