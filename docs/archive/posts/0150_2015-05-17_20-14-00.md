# Contract with MVNO
<div class="date">May 17, 2015 20:14</div>
<div id="post"><div id="body_show_ori">
Today, I contracted with MVNO called IIJmio.<br/><br/>I can communicate 3GB data per month, by paying about 1000 yen.<br/><br/>The reason for this contract is to watch dramas at a sports gym.<br/><br/>I soon went to the gym, and watched TBBT season 5 on Hulu while riding a static bike.<br/><br/>One episode of the drama required about 40MB data communication, so I can watch about 75 episodes.<br/><br/>Also, the fee of one episode is corresponding to about 13 yen.
</div></div>
