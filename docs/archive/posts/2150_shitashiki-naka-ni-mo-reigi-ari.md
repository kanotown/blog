# 【No. 2150】<strong><em>Shitashiki Naka ni mo Reigi Ari</em></strong> (親しき仲にも礼儀あり - Good Fences Make Good Neighbors)
<div class="date">Sep 10, 2023 16:16</div>
<div id="post"><div id="body_show_ori">
<strong><em>Shitashiki Naka ni mo Reigi Ari</em></strong><br/><br/>No matter how close the relationship, there are some etiquettes that should be observed.<br/><br/>The proverb, <strong><em>shitashiki naka ni mo reigi ari</em></strong> (親しき仲にも礼儀あり), expresses this notion.<br/><br/>Since <strong><em>shitashiki/shitashii</em></strong> (親しき/親しい) means "close," <strong><em>naka</em></strong> (仲) means "relationship," <strong><em>reigi</em></strong> (礼儀) means "etiquette," this proverb literally means "even in a close relationship, there is etiquette."<br/><br/>To build interpersonal relationships, we should treat everyone respectfully and be mindful of etiquette. <br/><br/>It is believed that this proverb derived from the "Analects" of Confucius.
</div></div>

<!-- more -->

<div id="post_ja"><div id="body_show_mo">
親しき仲にも礼儀あり<br/><br/>どんなに親しい間柄であっても、守るべき礼儀があります。<br/><br/>「親しき仲にも礼儀あり」ということわざは、このことを表しています。<br/><br/>「親しき/親しい」は "close"、「仲」は "relationship"、「礼儀」は "etiquette" を意味するので、「親しき仲にも礼儀あり」は文字どおり "even in a close relationship, there is etiquette" という意味になります。<br/><br/>良好な人間関係を築くために、どんな相手にも礼儀を欠かさないようにしましょう。<br/><br/>このことわざは、孔子の「論語」に由来すると言われています。
</div></div>
