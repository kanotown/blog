# 【No. 0398】Snow Shoveling
<div class="date">Jan 20, 2016 19:41</div>
<div id="post"><div id="body_show_ori">
It was sunny yesterday, but today it snowed heavily again.<br/><br/>For sake of safety, I shoveled the snow in various places.<br/><br/>Actually, I wear sandals through a year, but a lot of snow got through my sandals and it was cold.<br/><br/>I think I was idiot.<br/><br/>Also, I got tired from shoveling.<br/><br/>I searched for shoveling later, and it was found that the knack is to use whole your body, and not to twist your torso.
</div></div>

<!-- more -->

<div id="post_ja"><div id="body_show_mo">
雪かき<br/><br/>昨日は晴れましたが、今日はまた大雪でした。<br/><br/>そして安全のため、色々なところを雪かきしました。<br/><br/>実は私は一年中サンダルなのですが、雪かき中に雪が染みこんできて寒かったです。<br/><br/>馬鹿だったと思います。<br/><br/>そして雪かきはとても疲れました。<br/><br/>後で雪かきについて調べたところ、体をひねらず体全体を使うのがコツだそうです。
</div></div>
