<h1 id="subject_show">Snooze Button</h1>
<div class="date" hidden>Sep 5, 2015 10:42</div>
<div id="post"><div id="body_show_ori">
I use an snooze button of an alarm clock.<br/><br/>However, I found out that the snooze button derange your sleep cycle and biological clock, so it's not good for your health.<br/><br/>By using the snooze button, you will be "sleep inertia," which is something like spaced-out state.<br/><br/>Also, this will affect your work deeply over a day.<br/><br/>From now on, I will stop using the snooze button.
</div></div>
