# 【No. 2300】<strong><em>Jinsei Yama Ari Tani Ari</em></strong> (人生山あり谷あり - Life Has Its Ups and Downs)
<div class="date">Feb 7, 2024 13:58</div>
<div id="post"><div id="body_show_ori">
<strong><em>Jinsei Yama Ari Tani Ari</em></strong><br/><br/>In a long life, there are hardships as well as joys.<br/><br/>This idea is sometimes expressed as <strong><em>jinsei yama ari tani ari</em></strong> (人生山あり谷あり) in Japanese.<br/><br/>・<strong><em>Jinsei</em></strong> (人生) means "life."<br/>・<strong><em>Yama</em></strong> (山) means "mountain."<br/>・<strong><em>Ari</em></strong> (あり) means "there is."<br/>・<strong><em>Tani</em></strong> (谷) means "valley."<br/><br/>Therefore, the literal meaning of this phrase is "In life, there are mountains and valleys."<br/><br/>Here, "mountains" metaphorically represent hardships, and "valleys" represent enjoyable times.<br/><br/>It can be translated into English as "Life has its ups and downs."
</div></div>
